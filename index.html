Getting Started

  1. Open the application on your device.
  2. The login screen will be displayed.
  3. If you don’t have an account, click on Don’t have an account? to sign up.
  4. Fill out the sign-up form and submit it to create an account.
  5. After successful login, you can access all application features.

Application Features

Here’s how you can use the main features of the app:

  • Health Data Input: Add your daily health data, including sleep time, weight, steps, exercise burned calories, and medication reminders.
  • Delete Inputted Data: Swipe left on your input logs to delete any data.
